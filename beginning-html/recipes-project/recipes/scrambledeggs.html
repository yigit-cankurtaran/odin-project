Scrambled Eggs

Description

Eggs are a staple in cuisine all around the world. They are nutritious, cheap and have many ways to be cooked.

But this scrambled eggs recipe takes them to the next level for all your breakfast needs. Creamy, rich, and tasty, you will never crave for more than this. Ingredients are as follows:

  • 3 eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Any spices you like
  • Fresh chives
  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Pour preferred amount of milk in the eggs.
  3. Take small cubes of butter and put them in the mixture.
  4. Mix well using a fork.
  5. Put more butter into a pan and heat it up until liquid and golden.
  6. Pour the eggs into the pan and let them come into a creamy consistency.
  7. Plate the eggs and put salt, spices, and fresh chives on top of them.
  8. Enjoy your breakfast.
