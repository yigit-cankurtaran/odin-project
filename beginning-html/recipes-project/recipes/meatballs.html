Meatballs

Description

Meatballs are easy to make, packed with protein, nutrients and flavor.

After reading this recipe you'll also learn how to make a great batch of meatballs. First you'll need:

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Any spices you like
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Diced onions
  • Any cooking oil you prefer
  1. Pour all ingredients except the oil into a large bowl
  2. Knead it until it all comes together
  3. Form into little balls
  4. Pour cooking oil into a pan and let it come to a simmering heat
  5. Put meatballs in the pan
  6. Let it fry until brown
  7. Eat with any sauce you want
