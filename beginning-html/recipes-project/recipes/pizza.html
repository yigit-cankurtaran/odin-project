Pizza Crust

Description

This is a classic Italian pizza. It's one of my favorite foods to make and to eat.

To make a great pizza you need experience, skill, and quality ingredients. Ingredients such as:

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Basil leaves

The steps to use these ingredients go as follows:

  1. Mix flour, water, salt and some olive oil to make the dough
  2. Let dough rest for 3 to 4 hours
  3. Cut dough into balls and use a rolling pin to make them flat and circular
  4. Put as much as tomato sauce, cheese and some olive oil on top of the dough
  5. Bake at your preferred temperature for around 60 minutes.
  6. Enjoy the pizza you created.
